Torn Levelling Targets Tool

Torn Levelling Targets Tool provides optimal targets for Torn City players to level up efficiently. Select your level from the dropdown menu to view recommended targets and maximize experience points for quick progression.

Get started Learn more

To use the Torn Levelling Targets Tool, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the Settings page and enter your API key.
  2. Visit the Tools section to start using the tool.

By following these instructions, you will be able to utilize the Torn Levelling Targets Tool effectively and optimize your experience in Torn City.

Reaching level 15 in Torn City is crucial because it unlocks the ability to travel, which significantly improves the potential for making money. Players can engage in activities like selling flowers or plushies between countries, generating substantial profits. Additionally, level 15 marks the end of focusing on leveling up and transitioning to gym training with energy.

Leveling targets refer to specific individuals in the game who have relatively lower stats for their level, making them easier to defeat in combat. Players can attack these targets, leave them in the street, and repeat this process to earn experience points and level up quickly.

The Torn Levelling Targets Tool provides players with a list of optimal targets to attack based on their current level. This tool helps players maximize their experience points and advance through levels efficiently, ultimately contributing to reaching level 15 faster.
